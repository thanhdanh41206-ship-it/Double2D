**Apple Store Management System – Feature Description**

No.	Feature Name	        Description
1	User Authentication	    Allows administrators and staff to log in securely using a username and password.
2	Product Management	    Enables adding, updating, deleting, and viewing Apple products such as iPhone, iPad, MacBook, and accessories.
3	Category Management	    Organizes products into categories (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Accessories) for easier management and searching.
4	Inventory Management	Tracks product stock quantity, updates inventory after sales, and prevents selling out-of-stock items.
5	Customer Management	    Stores customer information including name, contact details, and purchase history.
6	Sales Management	    Handles product sales, calculates total price, applies discounts, and generates sales records.
7	Invoice Generation	    Automatically creates invoices for each transaction and saves them for future reference.
8	Search and Filter	    Allows users to search products by name, category, or price and filter results easily.
9	Revenue Report	        Generates daily, monthly, or yearly revenue reports to help analyze business performance.
10	Employee Management	    Manages employee information, roles, and access permissions within the system.
11	Data Persistence	    Saves all data to files or a database to ensure information is not lost when the system is closed.
12	System Exit \& Backup	Safely exits the system and supports data backup to prevent data loss.
